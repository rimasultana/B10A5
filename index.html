Donate for Flood at Noakhali, Bangladesh

Noakhali, Bangladesh has been significantly affected by floods, which have caused severe damage to homes, infrastructure, and people's livelihoods. Many families have been displaced, and there is an urgent need for relief in the form of food, clean water, medicine, and shelter. Local and international organizations, along with individuals, are stepping up to provide necessary aid.
